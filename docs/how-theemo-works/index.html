How Theemo Works

Theemo will work with tokens, passes them around from one tool to the other, but it will never understand them.

Example:

You will use tokens in figma, the way designers intend to. Next is to describe in Theemo what's their structure, how they are classified in order to prepare them to move them forward. You can think of it as the DSL to your tokens.

That most likely affects the sync part.
